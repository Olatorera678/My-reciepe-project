Salad reciepe

Ingredients

Lettuce

Tomato

Cucumber

Carrot

Boiled egg

Sweet corn

Cream

Steps

  • Wash everything thoroughly
  • chop lettuce/cabbage
  • Slice tomato and cucumber
  • Grate the carrot
  • Slice the boiled egg
  • Put everything in a bowl and mix gently
  • mix salad,lemon juice and salt(optional)
  • pour the dressing over the salad and serve gently

You can use chicken if you are rich
